Artisan Bakery Opens In Whitley Bay

After 12 years as a chef and having honed the craft of artisan bakery at the Black Sheep Bakery, Gibside and Newcastle Quayside markets, Robert Bell has now opened a new premises in Whitley Bay.

Bringing a taste of France to the North East, the bakery boasts a well stocked fromagerie and charcuterie in addition to croissants, pain au chocolat and speciality breads.

Open Tuesday to Saturday from 9am to 7pm and 9am to 12pm on a Sunday it has never been easier to enjoy artisan bread at its finest.
