Learn to Bake Artisan Bread

General Artisan baking course - an introduction to making real bread.

Topics covered include:

  • Mixing, kneading and hand shaping
  • Using sourdough starters and extended fermentation times to improve results
  • Creating flavoured breads and pizza
  • Getting the most out of your home oven

Classes take place in the bakery in a workshop environment with a maximum of four participants.
